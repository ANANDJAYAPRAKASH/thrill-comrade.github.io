About me

Hey there! I’m Anand Jayaprakash, a cybersecurity enthusiast and penetration tester with a knack for solving complex security challenges. Based in India, I dive deep into the digital world to uncover and fix vulnerabilities, ensuring systems stay resilient against threats.

Think of me as a cyber-sleuth, on a mission to safeguard networks, web applications, APIs, and more. My work is all about finding and exploiting security flaws, then crafting simple, actionable solutions to fortify defenses. From vulnerability assessments to full-blown penetration tests, I bring a hands-on, personalized approach to every project.

Along the way, I’ve earned certifications like CRTP, CEH, CAP, AZ-500, and AZ-900, completed HTB Prolab DANTE, and even secured a CVE for my vulnerability research. I’ve also had the privilege of working with industry leaders like EY and Bounteous X Accolite, helping organizations strengthen their cybersecurity posture.

Outside of hacking and securing systems, I love sharing my expertise through workshops, awareness sessions, and events.
